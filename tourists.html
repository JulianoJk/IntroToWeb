Welcome to Athens

Discover the timeless beauty of Athens, a city rich in history and culture. From ancient ruins to vibrant neighborhoods, Athens offers an array of attractions for every traveler.

Top Attractions

Acropolis

Marvel at the ancient glory of the Acropolis, home to the iconic Parthenon and breathtaking views over Athens.

Plaka

Wander through Plaka's picturesque streets, filled with neoclassical architecture, cozy cafes, and charming boutiques.

Services for Tourists

  • Guided tours of historical sites and museums.
  • Information centers offering maps, brochures, and event details.
  • Convenient rentals for bicycles, scooters, and cars to explore the city.
  • Multi-lingual support services for non-Greek speakers.
